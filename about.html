About Us

Trusted Building Materials Since 2011

Manyangwa Company Limited is a trusted Tanzanian manufacturer of high-quality building materials. Since 2011, we've been at the forefront of construction innovation, delivering products that combine durability, performance, and ease of application.

Our best Products include Skim Coat Wall Putty which is widely used by professionals to create smooth, flawless walls prior to painting, We also offer SpecTex Wall Texture, a stylish decorative finish that enhances any interior or exterior space, and Spectrum Paints, known for rich color, excellent coverage, and long-lasting protection. Whether you're a contractor, builder, or architect, our materials are engineered to exceed expectations.
